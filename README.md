# Power BI Visualizations & Reporting

This repository contains selected **Power BI dashboards and visualizations**
developed to support financial reporting, audit analysis, and operational
decision-making in an enterprise environment.

The focus of these projects is on **data modeling, audit transparency, and
executive-ready reporting**, rather than static visualization alone.

---

## Included Dashboards

### 1. Unrecorded Liability Audit Dashboard
- Built to support audit and accounting teams in identifying, tracking, and
  resolving unrecorded liability findings.
- Includes summary views, vendor analysis, accrual timing, service date lag,
  and cost center analysis.
- Emphasizes structured data modeling and drill-down analysis for audit review.

**Tools & Concepts**
- Power BI
- SQL-backed data models
- Dimensional modeling
- Audit-focused KPIs

**Artifact**
- `Audit_PBI_Report.pdf`

---

### 2. Fixed Asset & Depreciation Projections
- Developed to provide visibility into asset cost, net book value, additions,
  roll-offs, and depreciation timing across locations.
- Designed for finance and operations stakeholders to support planning and
  capital expenditure analysis.

**Tools & Concepts**
- Power BI
- Financial modeling concepts
- Historical trend analysis

**Artifact**
- `Depreciation_Projections_PBI.pdf`

---

### 3. Outstanding Gift Card Balances & Aging
- Created to monitor outstanding gift card balances, inactive orders, aging,
  and payment reconciliation.
- Supports audit, treasury, and accounting review of gift card exposure.

**Tools & Concepts**
- Power BI
- Data reconciliation
- Transaction-level drill-down

**Artifact**
- `Outstanding_Balances_PBI.pdf`

---

## Notes
- Dashboards are shared as **PDF exports and screenshots** due to data
  confidentiality.
- Visuals reflect real-world reporting use cases; underlying data has been
  excluded or anonymized.
